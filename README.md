# DeFi Risk Intelligence Aggregator

An open source web application that aggregates DeFi risk intelligence from every major feed into a single neutral view, presenting ratings verbatim and producing no synthesis or composite scoring of its own.

## Mission

DeFi risk intelligence exists but is fragmented across dozens of dashboards, rating services, and providers (BlockAnalitica, DeFiScan, DeFiPunk'd, CuratorWatch, Pharos, Credora, and others). No neutral layer aggregates these feeds in one place. This project fills that gap.

## Principles

- **Neutral**: No composite scoring. Feed ratings shown verbatim only.
- **Open source**: Developed in the open from day one (MIT license).
- **Community-correctable**: Data layer accepts corrections via PRs.
- **Ethereum-native**: Primary focus on Ethereum mainnet protocols.

## Coverage

At launch, covers the top 20 Ethereum DeFi protocols by TVL.
